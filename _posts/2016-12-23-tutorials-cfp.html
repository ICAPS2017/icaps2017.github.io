ICAPS 2017 Tutorials

Call for Workshop Proposals

The Organizing Committee for the 27th International Conference on Automated Planning and Scheduling (ICAPS 2017) invites proposals for its Tutorial Program.Â  Tutorials offer the attendees first-hand experience with state-of- the-art trends in research and applications by leading experts. Registration is free for ICAPS participants.

We welcome proposals in all areas related to AI planning and scheduling (P&S), and highly encourage typically under-represented topics, such as domain modelling techniques and P&S applications. We especially welcome topics from outside the ICAPS community that merge P&S with other areas such as robotics, autonomous agents, and knowledge representation. Tutorials may include either introductory or advanced topics, providing either breadth or depth into into P&S.

We expect majority of tutorials to be half-day but quarter-day (90 minutes) tutorials are also possible. Tutorial Proposals

Tutorial Submissions should include the title, type (advanced or introductory), summary, outline, and expected length (quarter or half-day) of the tutorial, as well as a short CV of the proposer(s), including a list of significant publications and/or prior teaching experience (given tutorials etc.).

Submission Procedure

Tutorial proposals should be submitted by email, in PDF, to both the Tutorial Chairs with subject "Tutorial Proposal for ICAPS-17" no later than February 1, 2017. Organizers of tutorials will be notified by email of the acceptance or rejection of their proposal by February 20, 2017.

Summary of Important Dates

  • Deadline for submission of workshop proposals: February 1, 2016.
  • Notification of workshop acceptance: February 20, 2016.
  • Tutorial dates: June 19 and 20, 2017.

Doctoral Consortium

Call for Participation

Submit

The Organizing Committee of the International Conference on Automated Planning and Scheduling (ICAPS 2017) invites Ph.D. students to apply for the Doctoral Consortium. The aim of the Doctoral Consortium is to provide a forum for students to discuss their Ph.D. work-in-progress with other students and senior researchers working in similar areas, receive advice and mentoring by senior members of the field regarding careers and research skills, and generally to accelerate the cohesion of new researchers with the ICAPS community and nurture the future of the field.

The Doctoral Consortium will be a workshop on June 18th, one day prior to the main conference. The DC participants will also present posters during the main ICAPS conference, in a session open to all ICAPS participants.

Students accepted in the Doctoral Consortium are granted free conference registration and are eligible for partial financial support and to apply for a special reduced-rate CMU lodging option.

Application Procedure

Applicants should submit a single zip, tar, or PDF file that contains:

  • A dissertation abstract (not a paper) of at most 4 pages, using AAAI style.
  • A CV.
  • A short recommendation letter from their Ph.D. supervisor verifying the applicantâ€™s enrollment in a PhD program and summarizing why the DC will especially benefit the applicant.

For any questions or problems, please contact the DC chairs.

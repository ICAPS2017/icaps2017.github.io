ICAPS-2017 Venue Information

ICAPS 2017 will take place in the Gates Center for Computer Science and Hillman Center for Future Generation Technologies at Carnegie Mellon. The registration room is outside GHC 4405. There will be signs throughout the Gates-Hillman Center leading you there.
You can find a map of Carnegie Mellon University here. The conference will take place in building 9A and 9B on the map, circled in red on the smaller map below.

Getting to CMU

Bus

The Pittsburgh International Airport is 22 miles from Carnegie Mellon. The Pittsburgh Port Authority runs the convenient 28X bus route from the airport. The 28X departs every 30 minutes from the airport, and the fare is $3.75. The route goes through downtown Pittsburgh and the last stop is at CMU, taking about an hour. The last stop is directly across the road from CMU, at the intersection of Forbes and Morewood.

Shuttle

An alterative to the bus is the SuperShuttle. One-way fare from the airport to campus ranges from $27.00 to $36.00. For reservations, contact Super Shuttle at 1-800-258-3826.

Taxi

Taxi service is also available from the airport, and it costs about $50-$60 one way plus tip. There are typically taxi cabs outside of the airport, but if you need to call for a cab, you can call Yellow Cab (412-321-8100).
